Overview Word Pairing: Category Match (“the app”) is a word game by Snickerdoodlez (“we”, “our”). We don’t want your life story. We only use data where it’s needed to run the app, improve it, and handle game services. Data We May Collect Depending on your settings and which services are enabled, the app may collect: • Analytics & Diagnostics: To fix bugs and improve the app, we use Google Firebase services (Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring). This collects app usage data, crash reports, performance metrics, and device information (like OS version and device model). • Google Play Games Data: If you sign in with Google Play Games, we process data related to your profile (username, profile picture) and game activity (scores, achievements, leaderboards). • Advertising-related signals: If ads are enabled, we may collect consent status and limited ad identifiers as permitted by platform rules to show and measure ads. Ads & Consent If the app shows ads, we use Google’s consent tools (where required) so you can manage ad personalization choices. How We Use Data • To operate and maintain the app and its features. • To enable Google Play Games services like leaderboards and achievements. • To fix bugs and improve gameplay and stability. • To show ads (if enabled) and measure their performance. • To comply with legal and platform policies. Sharing We do not sell your personal data. We share limited data with Google, our service provider for analytics, crash reporting, performance monitoring, and game services, strictly for the purposes described above. Your Choices • You can manage consent options (when available) from within the app. • You can manage your Google Play Games profile and data through your Google account settings. • You can limit ad tracking/identifiers using your device settings. • You can uninstall the app at any time to stop data collection. Children’s Privacy The app is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us at privacy@snickerdoodlez.online and we’ll take appropriate steps.
