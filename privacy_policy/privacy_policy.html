Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 21, 2025 • Contact: privacy@snickerdoodlez.online

This page is styled to match the Snickerdoodlez “control center” vibe: high-contrast tiles, neon accents, and zero nonsense. If you have privacy questions, email privacy@snickerdoodlez.online.

Overview The short version

Snickerdoodlez (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is a word game. We don’t want your life story. We only use data where it’s needed to run the app, improve it, and (if enabled) show ads.

Data We May Collect depends on features

Depending on your settings and which services are enabled, the app may collect:

  • App usage / diagnostics (crash reports, performance metrics).
  • Device information (OS version, device model) for compatibility and debugging.
  • Advertising-related signals (only if ads are enabled), such as consent status and limited ad identifiers where permitted by law and platform rules.

Ads & Consent you control it

If the app shows ads, we use Google’s consent tools (where required) so you can manage ad personalization choices. In some regions, there may be no consent options to display.

Questions about consent? Email privacy@snickerdoodlez.online.

How We Use Data why it exists

  • To operate and maintain the app.
  • To fix bugs and improve gameplay and stability.
  • To show ads (if enabled) and measure ad performance.
  • To comply with legal requirements and platform policies.

Sharing who might get what

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with service providers that help run the app, such as analytics, crash reporting, or advertising partners, strictly for the purposes described above.

Your Choices power buttons

  • You can manage consent options (when available) from within the app.
  • You can limit ad tracking/identifiers using your device settings.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to stop data collection.

Children’s Privacy no creepy stuff

The app is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us at privacy@snickerdoodlez.online and we’ll take appropriate steps.

Contact one door

For privacy questions, requests, or concerns, contact:
privacy@snickerdoodlez.online
