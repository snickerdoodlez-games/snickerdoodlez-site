Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2025 • Contact: privacy@snickerdoodlez.online

Overview

Word Pairing: Category Match (“the app”) is a word game by Snickerdoodlez (“we”, “our”). We only use data where it’s needed to run the app and improve it. If you have any privacy questions, please email privacy@snickerdoodlez.online.

Data We May Collect

Depending on your settings and which services are enabled, the app may collect:

  • Analytics & Diagnostics: To fix bugs and improve the app, we use Google Firebase services (Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring). This collects app usage data, crash reports, performance metrics, and device information (like OS version and device model).
  • Advertising-related signals: If ads are enabled, we may collect consent status and limited ad identifiers as permitted by platform rules to show and measure ads.

How We Use Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the app and its features.
  • To fix bugs and improve gameplay and stability.
  • To show ads (if enabled) and measure their performance.
  • To comply with legal and platform policies.

Ads & Consent

If the app shows ads, we use Google’s consent tools (where required) so you can manage ad personalization choices.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited data with Google, our service provider for analytics, crash reporting, and performance monitoring, strictly for the purposes described above.

Your Choices

You have control over your data. You can:

  • Manage consent options (when available) from within the app.
  • Limit ad tracking/identifiers using your device settings.
  • Uninstall the app at any time to stop data collection.

Children’s Privacy: The app is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us at privacy@snickerdoodlez.online and we’ll take appropriate steps.
